About Us

A Philadelphia firm shaping the future of global advisory

Israa Philly International is a multidisciplinary international consulting and programme advisory firm headquartered in the Greater Philadelphia area. Our flagship practice deploys Agentic AI to coordinate complex international programmes — providing real-time intelligence, autonomous task management, and strategic decision support to governments, development organisations, and global enterprises.

Beyond technology, we bring deep expertise in green energy, import/export trade, agricultural commodities, and sales strategy — giving clients a single, trusted partner across the full spectrum of international engagement.

Whether you're coordinating a multi-country programme, entering a frontier market, or sourcing international commodities, we bring the intelligence, insight, and connections to make it happen.
